== Description ==
BumpIn Twitter Connect plugin lets your visitors view your tweets and tweets made about you without leaving the site. Twitter widget fully integrates your twitter accuont with your website/blog. It not only increases your followers on twitter but also the time spent by the visitors on your site and dcreases the bounce rate.
Following is the list of features of the plugin.

Users can follow you in just one click without leaving your website.

Live twitter stream from your twitter account.

Separate section for Tweets about you.

Users can tweet about the article/page through an automatically filled text form.

Number of followers.

Users can follow you on twitter.

Search Tweets without leaving the site.


== Installation ==

Follow the procedure to install Twitter Connect for you blog/site.

1. Download bumpin-twitter.zip
2. Unzip bumpin-twitter.zip and upload `BumpIn_Twitter.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. Drag Drop the 'Twitter Connect' widget to your sidebar according to your need.(goto *Presentation-> widgets*)
5. Customize and Register your widget(FREE!) by visiting socialbar.BumpIn.com (If you have not already registered)
6. Paste the code you got from BumpIn website in the text box and click the "Save Changes" button
7. Verify your email address used for registering at socialbar.BumpIn.com and set your password by clicking on the link sent to your email account
8. Re-customize your widget by logging in at socialbar.BumpIn.com using your email address as your login name
9. If you re-customize the widget from the SocialBar admin panel, the changes would automatically be reflected on your site/blog
10. If you forgot your password you can reset it from http://socialbar.bumpin.com/site/forgot_password/ by verifying your email id
